Findings

We offer a range of services to help you navigate the housing market:

  • Consultation with housing market experts
  • Guidance on financing and budgeting
  • Strategies for finding the right property
  • Workshops on first-time home buying

Key Insights

Based on our research, the following insights have emerged regarding the current housing market:

  • The demand for affordable housing continues to outpace supply.
  • Many buyers are facing financial constraints due to rising interest rates.
  • Government initiatives aimed at first-time buyers have had mixed results.
