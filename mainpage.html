Buying Under Pressure

Introduction

Since the last 10 years, housing prices in Sydney have not just increased; they have doubled. This and various other factors have contributed to people changing their way of life and moving out of their houses. The basic necessity of having a roof over their head seems impossible for quite a significant number of people, especially in Sydney.
